About Us

D&D Associates delivers audit, taxation, GST, accounting, ROC compliance, advisory and financial consulting services with professionalism and integrity.

Meet the Founder

CA Sadhana Kumari

Representing a new generation of Chartered Accountants, CA Sadhana Kumari brings together academic excellence, professionalism, and a client-first approach. She is committed to helping individuals, startups, and businesses navigate taxation, audit, compliance, and financial decision-making with clarity and integrity.

Her journey began at Kendriya Vidyalaya No. 2, Gaya, where she built a strong academic foundation. She later graduated from the University of Delhi before earning the prestigious Chartered Accountant qualification. Through D&D Associates, her vision is to deliver dependable, ethical, and practical financial solutions while building lasting relationships based on trust and excellence.
